Welcome to Squonk Labs

Squonk labs is where we experiment with things prior to inclusion in the Squonk Computational notebook. This includes new features and enhancements to existing features. It gives users a chance to see what's coming and to provide input into how things should work.

These pages are usually simple HTML/Javascript pages free of any of the Computational Notebook infrastructure. This provides a simpler and faster developement environment, but the aim is to do this in a way that allows the components (usually just JavaScript and CSS) to to dropped into the Computational Notebook with no, or only minmal, changes.
